🔺 Why It's a Triangle (Mathematical Constraint)

The key insight: You cannot have high variation (MAD) when the average is extreme.

Maximum MAD = min(1 + average, 1 - average)

(-1, 0)

COMPLETE REJECTION

Everyone strongly dislikes it
No variation possible

(+1, 0)

COMPLETE CONSENSUS

Everyone strongly likes it
No variation possible

(0, 1)

MAXIMUM POLARIZATION

Half love it, half hate it
Maximum possible disagreement

Examples of the Constraint:

  • Average = -1: All responses must be -1 → MAD = 0 (no variation possible)
  • Average = -0.5: Maximum MAD = 0.5 (some at -1, fewer at higher values)
  • Average = 0: Maximum MAD = 1.0 (half at -1, half at +1)
  • Average = +0.8: Maximum MAD = 0.2 (responses clustered near +1)
