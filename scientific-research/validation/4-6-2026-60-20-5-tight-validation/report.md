This is the **controlled companion** to [`4-6-2026-60-20-5-validation`](../4-6-2026-60-20-5-validation/report.md).
That run used a deliberately *loose* corpus (within-synth cosine 0.73–0.90) and the

This run tests that hypothesis on a *tight* corpus and confirms it.

---

## 1. Objective

Re-run the production synthesis mechanism on the **same structure** but with
realistic, tightly-worded paraphrases, to isolate whether the loose run's
synth fragmentation was the mechanism or the corpus. Expectation: if it was the
corpus, the tight run recovers all 12 synths cleanly.

## 2. Dataset

```
3 topics  ×  4 synths per topic  ×  5 paraphrases per synth  =  60 options
```
Identical ground-truth structure to the loose run (same 12 synth labels, same
topics). The only change is **wording width**: each synth's 5 paraphrases share
a strong common phrasing (realistic restatements) rather than maximal lexical
variation. Source: `scripts/seedSynthBenchmark.healthyTight.json`. Per-statement
labels in [`statements.json`](./statements.json).

**Embedding-space geometry** (raw 1536-d cosine, shipped vectors):

| Pair class | mean | min | max |
|---|---|---|---|
| within-synth | **0.972** | **0.933** | 0.997 |
| cross-synth, same topic | 0.683 | 0.567 | 0.826 |
| cross-topic | 0.610 | 0.501 | 0.741 |

The bands are now **cleanly separated** (within-synth min 0.933 ≫ cross-synth max
0.826), and **118/120 within-synth pairs clear the 0.94 auto-accept band** —
exactly the realistic regime the loose corpus (max 0.896) failed to reach.

### 2.1 Corpus construction & provenance

LLM-authored synthetic sentences (Claude), 2026-06-04. Still synthetic, so the
standard threat to validity applies (cleaner than real deliberation — no typos,
drift, or adversarial restatement). The point of *this* corpus is that its
within-synth cosine is in the realistic ≥0.93 range, unlike the intentionally
loose companion run. A clean pass here is necessary, not sufficient, evidence;
robustness still needs production-derived corpora.

## 5. Verdict

**Synth mechanism: PASS (clean).** On a realistic-tight corpus the production
path (`bulkClusterByEmbedding` + `twoTierJudge`, with the two fixes from commit
`7465c9342`) recovers the ground-truth synth structure **exactly** — 12 pure
synths, no merges, no drops, no overlap, no LLM calls. Comparing to the loose
companion run isolates the cause cleanly:

| | loose corpus (`…-validation`) | tight corpus (this run) |
|---|---|---|
| within-synth cosine | 0.73–0.90 (below auto-accept) | 0.93–0.997 (above auto-accept) |
| synths recovered pure | 6 / 12 | **12 / 12** |
| options assigned | 47 / 60 | **60 / 60** |
| `eat-veg` vs `portion` split | ✅ | ✅ |
| judge LLM calls | 63 | **0** |

The loose run's fragmentation was therefore a **corpus-realism artifact**: when
paraphrases sit below the auto-accept band, every member is LLM-judged and the
medoid-anchored judge occasionally splits a true paraphrase; when they sit in the
realistic ≥0.93 range, the cosine tier handles them and the result is exact. The
mechanism is trustworthy for synth recovery. (Topic-level grouping is a separate,
still-open diagnostic — see §6.)

## 6. Notes & scope

  recovery is out of scope for the synth mechanism validated here and is the
  natural next investigation.
