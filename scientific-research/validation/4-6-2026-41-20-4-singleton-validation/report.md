## 1. Objective

Test that a **single statement that belongs to neither cluster** stays
**unclustered** (archetype C "lone singleton" / F "noise" in
`DESIGNING-TEST-CORPORA.md`): the pipeline should recover the real synths and
**not force the lone statement into one of them**.

## 2. Dataset

```
2 topics × 5 synths × 4 paraphrases = 40, + 1 off-topic statement = 41
```

| Topic | Synths (5 each, 4 paraphrases) |
|---|---|
| physical-fitness | daily-cardio · strength-training · flexibility-stretching · active-commuting · team-sports |
| personal-finance | budget-monthly · emergency-fund · pay-off-debt · invest-retirement · cut-subscriptions |
| _(outside)_ | **off-topic-noise** — *"Photosynthesis is the process by which plants convert sunlight into chemical energy."* (1 statement, `role: noise`) |

Two deliberately distinct domains (fitness, finance) so the clusters are clean;
the lone statement is off-topic, intended to stay out. Tight wording.
LLM-authored synthetic (Claude). Labels in [`statements.json`](./statements.json).

## 3. Procedure & parameters

Seed (41/41 embeddings) → `bulkClusterByEmbedding` (ε=0.45, seed 42) →
`twoTierJudge` → write docs. `autoRejectBand` 0.60, `autoAcceptBand` 0.94,
`maxLlmCalls` 2000. Manifest in [`results.json`](./results.json).

## 4. Results

Scored by [`score.mjs`](../score.mjs):

| Metric | Expected | Observed | Pass |
|---|---|---|---|
| Synths recovered pure & exact size (4) | 10 | **9** | ❌ |
| Topic-clusters | 2 | **2** (physical-fitness, personal-finance) | ✅ |
| Topic purity | one topic each | **both clean** | ✅ |
| Options in >1 synth (overlap) | 0 | **0** | ✅ |
| **Lone statement kept out of synths** | 0 swallowed | **1 swallowed** (into daily-cardio) | ❌ |
| Options assigned | 40 | 41 | ❌ |

**`score.mjs` verdict: ❌ FAIL — 14/18 checks.** The 40 clustered options were
recovered well: **9 of 10 synths pure and exact-size, both topics clean, zero
overlap** — and notably the two distinct-domain topics separated cleanly (unlike
the same-domain health corpus). The failure is the test's point: **the lone
off-topic statement was absorbed into the `daily-cardio` synth** (making it
5 members, 80% pure) rather than left unclustered.

## 5. Verdict & finding

**FAIL on the property under test: the bulk path does NOT reliably leave a lone
statement unclustered.** This was robust across three different outlier
statements (cosine 0.65, 0.69, 0.59 to the nearest synth) and an ε sweep:

- The lone statement is absorbed not by the cosine noise-reassignment alone but
  by **UMAP→DBSCAN proximity** — DBSCAN runs in the UMAP-reduced space, where the
  outlier sat within ε of the `daily-cardio` core, so it was clustered directly
  (noise=0). The two-tier judge then **kept** it, because the host cluster was
  otherwise ≥80% coherent (keep-cluster-as-is rule).
- The ε sweep shows the tension: at ε=0.30 the outlier *is* noise, but the looser
  real synths (flexibility, team-sports) fragment too; at ε≥0.35 every synth
  forms but the outlier is absorbed. **No ε both recovers the 10 synths and
  leaves the lone statement out** on this corpus — the outlier is not a strong
  enough UMAP outlier (it sits about as far as the looser real synths).

The archetype-C expectation ("singleton stays an unclustered raw option") holds
only when the lone statement is a *strong* outlier in the reduced space. For a
moderately-distinct statement under a broad question (every plausible answer sits
~0.6–0.7 cosine to the fitness synths because the question context is shared),
the bulk path pulls it into the nearest cluster. This is a genuine limitation to
record, parallel to the eps-granularity findings elsewhere.
